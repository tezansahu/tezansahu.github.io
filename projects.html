MY PROJECTS...


Every project is an opportunity to learn, to figure out problems and challenges, to invent and reinvent.

Over the course of time, I have been a part of several projects and hackathons. Following is a list of projects that I have completed or have contributed to:

Hobby Projects

  • Attendance System using Face Recognition:

    For the Institute Technical Summer Project-2018, our team (TechnoVision) intended to build an Attendance Recording Bot that could use face recognition to mark the attendance of students while they are sitting for the lecture. We created an app for the students so that they get to check their attendance, which would be updated after the bot records their attendance. Another app, for the professor, to control the bot using BlueTooth has also been developed by us. The entire coding was done in python on a RaspberryPi.
    Follow this link to check out the working of our project.

  • Reading Project On Machine Learning:

    This was done as a part of the Summer of Science 2018 under the Mathematics & Physics Club, IIT Bombay. I completed the Machine Learning Specialization course offered on Coursera by the University of Washington. During this period, I referred to several research papers and other courses on Machine Learning and Deep Learning Concepts & Algorithms. Through this project, I got a hands-on experience of working with Scikit-Learn, GraphLab Create, TensorFlow and Octave.
    The project report can be accessed through this link.

Hackathons

  • ETHIndia Hackathon 2018 (Bengaluru):

    This was my first hackathon and it was centered around creating a Decentralized Application (DApp) using frameworks and technologies on Ethereum. I was a part of a three-member team along with Akash Kumar and Deep Karman Pal Singh. We tried to design a DApp to facilitate Land Registry by tokenizing land and later use it as a collateral for lending/borrowing purposes. We were declared winners of the Sponsor Prize offered by Lendroid.
