About Beyond Code

Critical Strategies to Balance Technical & Soft Skills

You've spent countless hours learning how to code, analyze data, and build models.
But do you know how to present your findings, persuade your audience, and solve real-world problems?

If not, you're missing out on a huge opportunity to grow your career and make an impact.

80% of data science projects fail to deliver value – not due to a lack of technical skills, but rather, the lack of balance between technical proficiency & soft skills such as communication, collaboration, and leadership.

If you want to avoid being part of this statistic, you need to have both kinds of 'muscles to flex' for an impactful career. Imagine if you could influence stakeholders, lead projects, and create a personal brand that sets you apart from the crowd.

That's exactly what "Beyond Code" will teach you, with critical & actionable strategies to level up your career as a data scientist, analyst, or engineer.

Authored by Tezan Sahu, a Data & Applied Scientist at Microsoft, and an Alumnus of IIT Bombay, this comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know to excel in this industry.

From core concepts like balancing technical depth & breadth, data-driven mindset, creativity, customer obsession & data storytelling, to broader themes like productivity, personal branding, leadership, and much more, this book has it all!

Whether you are a data scientist, data analyst, data engineer, software developer, or program manager – a seasoned pro or just starting out – this practical guide definitely has something in store for you!
